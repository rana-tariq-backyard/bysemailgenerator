Copy to Clipboard is disabled intially so:

  1. Enter your full name in both English and Arabic.
  2. Provide your job title in both languages.
  3. Add your phone number (starting with the country code, e.g., 966).
  4. Click "Generate Signature" to see a preview.
  5. Use the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy the signature.
Once copied, paste the signature into your email client settings.
